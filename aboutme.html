Hi, I'm Gabriel

My home is McKinney, Texas

Early Years

I was born on a snowy, cold morning in Nacogdoches, Texas. Which was weird because it hardly snows in Texas, let alone east texas.

And it was may...

Little did I know though, that's when it would all start. I spent the first twelve years of my life being a child, then abruptly, at thirteen, I was teenager. That went on for years and years until eighteen, I was this weird hybrid of a teenager and adult. Boy, life hit me like a freight train right in the face with that one.

The Other Years

Once I hit twenty, I was finally out of that crazy period where I was an adult and a child. The constant teasing because of it was finally over and I was a man. That was three years ago and not a whole lot has changed since then. I still get teased but thats for unrelated issues.

Inbetween then and now, a lot has happened. More than I can fit on this page. If you'd like to to learn more about myself and my life, you can buy my autobiography
